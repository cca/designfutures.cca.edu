PAST | Farid Rakun

Nov 19 7:30 - 8:30pm Online Event

We were punks once...

This lecture will encompass the reflections of Farid Rakun, of Jakarta's artists collective ruangrupa and the informal education platform Gudskul, on collective practices and working both outside and inside systems of power. From The Kuda (2012) to the formation of Gudskul (2018), and the making of the upcoming documenta fifteen (2022), the lecture will explore work beyond institutional critique, towards new forms of institutional aesthetics/practice.

A webinar link will be sent 30-45 minutes prior to the beginning of the event via email.
